[_HistoGAN: Controlling Colors of GAN-Generated and Real Images via Color Histograms_](https://arxiv.org/abs/2011.11731) with Michael Brown and Mahmoud Afifi has been accepted at [CVPR 2021](https://cvpr2021.thecvf.from).  Congratulations and thanks to the student, Mahmoud Afifi, and his supervisor Michael Brown for the fruitful collaboration!
