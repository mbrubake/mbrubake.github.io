Several new workshop papers and preprints are now available on arXiv!
 - [_CAMS: Color-Aware Multi-Style Transfer_](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.13920) by Mahmoud Afifi, Abdullah Abuolaim, Mostafa Hussien, Marcus A. Brubaker and Michael S. Brown, preprint.
 - [_Zero-shot Learning with Class Description Regularization_](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.16108) by Shayan Kousha and Marcus A. Brubaker at CVPR Workshop on Fine-Grained Visual Categorization, 2021.
 - [_Continuous Latent Process Flows_](https://arxiv.org/abs/2106.15580) by Ruizhi Deng, Marcus A. Brubaker, Greg Mori and Andreas Lehrmann at ICML Workshop on Time Series, 2021.
 - [_Manifold Density Estimation via Generalized Dequantization_](https://arxiv.org/abs/2102.07143) by James A. Brofos, Marcus A. Brubaker and Roy R. Lederman at ICML Workshop on Invertible Neural Networks, Normalizing Flows, and Explicit Likelihood Models, 2021.
